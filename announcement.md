## Aptitude Arena – Event Announcement

**Title**: Aptitude Arena – Battle of Wits  
**Type**: Aptitude challenge (Written Round + Live Game)  
**Target**: Juniors (all branches/years as decided by department)

---

### Event description

Do you enjoy solving puzzles, logical questions, and tricky maths?  
**Aptitude Arena** is a two-stage event designed to test and sharpen your **quantitative**, **logical reasoning**, and **verbal ability** skills in a fun, competitive way.

- **Round 1 – Written Aptitude Test (individual)**  
  A 45-minute MCQ test with questions from quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, and verbal ability.

- **Round 2 – Aptitude Arena Game (team-based)**  
  Shortlisted students will be grouped into teams and will battle it out on a **live interactive game board** projected on screen. Teams choose questions from different categories and score points for correct answers.

### Rounds and rules (short version for poster/WhatsApp)

- **Round 1 – Written Test**
  - 30 MCQs (Quant, Logical, Verbal).  
  - Time: **45 minutes**.  
  - +2 marks for correct answers, no negative marking.  
  - Top performers are shortlisted for Round 2.

- **Round 2 – Aptitude Arena Game**
  - Shortlisted students are divided into **three balanced teams**.  
  - Teams take turns choosing questions from different aptitude categories on a **projected game board**.  
  - Correct answers earn points; the team with the **highest score** wins.
